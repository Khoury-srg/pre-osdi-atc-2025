2025 Pre-OSDI/ATC Workshop

Northeastern Systems Research Group is organizing a half-day pre-OSDI/ATC workshop on the Northeastern campus, featuring OSDI/ATC practice talks and informal discussions to foster community and build connections.

  • Registration:https://forms.gle/4ticww1PdCkxuPtg7
  • Date: July 6 (Sunday), 2025
  • Time: 1PM-6PM
  • Location: Northeastern University, West Village H Room 102, 440 Huntington Ave, Boston, MA 02115

    (About 16-minute walk from OSDI/ATC venue; Bus 39 - Huntington Ave @ Forsyth Way stop; Green line - Northeastern University station; Orange line - Ruggles station).
